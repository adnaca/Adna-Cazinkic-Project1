SCARF LOVER

New 2019 Fashion Oversized Luxury Pure Silk Scarf, November 2019

Silk Scarfs! I am crazy about silk scarfs these days. Winter will be here before you know it, and that means it's time to start thinking about layering up. Scarves are obviously a winter wardrobe essential, but with so many different styles and designs to pick from, it can be hard to choose the right one for you. That's why I rounded up the absolute best fall scarves for women that range from lighter pieces to heavier shawls. There are classic options like oversized, silk, and plaid, as well as few surprising pieces, including one that comes with a secret zipper pocket (trust me, you're going to love it).

Remember: A great scarf is a fun way of mixing in bold prints and autumnal colors into your already stylish outfits.

ISTANBUL MODEST FASHION WEEK

An online Muslim fashion retailer, and the Islamic Fashion and Design Council, December 30, 2019

Dont miss! There have been Muslim-fashion events before, but none as international as this one. Brands and influencers from around the world, over 25 different countries, attended Istanbul Modest Fashion Week. Istanbul was a well-chosen location for this event. 2/3 of Turkish women wear the hijab, leading to a distinct fashion and an extremely lucrative market. The fashion in Turkey is distinct. This event could have included only Turkish brands and still have done as well as it did. But inviting influencers and brands from around the world ushered in a significant turning point in the Muslim fashion industry. Like the aim of modest fashion platform Amaliah, it siginifed a move to unite the global Muslim fashion industry.
